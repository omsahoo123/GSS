# **App Name**: Grameen Swasthya Setu

## Core Features:

- Multilingual Video Consultation: Enable video consultations between rural patients and doctors, supporting multiple regional languages.
- Offline Digital Health Records: Allow patients and doctors to access and update health records even without an active internet connection. Once connectivity resumes the records will automatically synchronize with the cloud.
- Real-time Medicine Availability: Provide real-time updates on medicine stock levels at local pharmacies to avoid unnecessary trips.
- AI-Powered Symptom Checker: Integrate an AI-powered symptom checker, optimized to function effectively even with low bandwidth. This tool should guide patients towards appropriate care and consultation options.
- Appointment Scheduling: Allow patients to schedule and manage telemedicine appointments with available doctors.
- Telemedicine Platform: Enable staff at the civil hospital with ability to make notes in the system as well as manage prescriptions digitally.
- Role-Based Login and Dashboards: Implement role-based login for Health department, doctor, patient, pharmacist store, and data store, with customized dashboards for each role.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Calm blue (#75A9FF) to evoke trust and health.
- Background color: Soft, desaturated blue (#E8F0FE) for a clean and calming backdrop.
- Accent color: Muted green (#90EE90) to symbolize growth and healing, providing contrast for calls-to-action.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, combining modern style and a little warmth.
- Use clear, universally understandable icons to represent medical information and app features.
- Prioritize a simple and intuitive layout to accommodate users with varying levels of technical literacy.
- Use subtle animations for loading screens and transitions to enhance user experience without being distracting.
